COMMUNITY

MASJID

The mosque committee oversees the spiritual activities of the club, including Quranic sessions, maintenance of the school's mosque, organization of Tajweed and Sirâh competitions, and hosting conferences featuring distinguished Moroccan scholars during the event "FI RIHAB ALHABIB."

SOCIAL

The social committee organizes all activities with the theme of volunteering and charity. Its operations can be in the form of awareness days, caravans charitable, etc.

CULTURE

The cultural committee organizes any event or action having a cultural background and artistic. This can take the form of event days, conferences or any other suitable format.

HISTORY

  • May 20, 2014

    CSA Creation
    The Creation of Sawaid Al Amal as the first club in ENSAM CASABLANCA.

  • 2014-2015

    The school prayer room
    The club offers as leader and responsible for the project of fitting out the school prayer room, a project that he carried out with seriousness and motivation and in which he demonstrated his commitment and dynamism.

  • 2014-2015

    S4S
    The club continued to expand the scope of its activities aimed at develop the skills and deepen the knowledge of ENSAMiens at the level of his studies, by organizing language courses (Spanish and Japanese) as well as support course as part of the S4S project.

  • January 24, 2016

    First Charity Caravan
    The story of our first charity caravan, organized on January 24, 2016 in Ouaouizeght, is imbued with compassion and solidarity towards the inhabitants of the remote villages of the Atlas.

  • 2016-2017

    The Word Master
    A cultural event which offers participants the opportunity to highlight their qualities of argumentation, expression and public speaking. The event allows everyone to express themselves freely, whether in French, English or classical Arabic.

  • 2016-2017

    ENSAMian Diversity
    The "Sawaid Al Amal" club also organizes a cultural day under the theme “ENSAMienne Diversity” as part of its “Sawaid à l'Africaine” whose aim is to share and exchange African traditions in order to create a cultural fusion and to facilitate the culture of cohabitation and solidarity between foreign students in Morocco.

  • 2017-2018

    Creation of a Reading Club
    Club created as part of a French course, constituting an extracurricular activity at benefit of college students.

  • 2017-2018

    Creation of a Science Club
    Club which aims to teach children while playing, always favoring manipulation directly by the child. Thus his interest and curiosity are constantly awakened. Students learn new things without even realizing it and while having fun with it different electrical, mechanical and thermal manipulations.

  • 2023-2024

    Creation of Tinirt Library
    Club which aims to teach children while playing, always favoring manipulation directly by the child. Thus his interest and curiosity are constantly awakened. Students learn new things without even realizing it and while having fun with it different electrical, mechanical and thermal manipulations.

  • October 23, 2022

    Douar Mestour Caravan
    The caravan aiming to bring hope and help to the inhabitants of Douar Mestour, a community in the commune of Tighadouine (province of Marrakech). In a region where access to electricity is limited, we undertook to install solar panels to provide energy to a well, ensuring access continuous access to drinking water for 51 families in need.

  • 2023-2024

    Dima F'Lbal
    The outing to the forest, organized for the benefit of the residents of the retirement home, was our last action organized under the name “Dima F'Lbal”, a memorable day marked by solidarity and intergenerational exchange.
