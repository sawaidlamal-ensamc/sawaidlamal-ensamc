ABOUT US

The SAWAID ALAMAL Club, the first club founded at ENSAM Casablanca, is a pioneering student initiative dedicated to humanitarian, cultural, social and spiritual commitment. We organize solidarity events and actions aimed at strengthening the personal development of our members, while encouraging solidarity, mutual aid and open-mindedness. Our mission is to train responsible and committed engineering students, providing them with a space to develop their skills while contributing to the well-being of the community, because we believe that leadership is expressed as much through professional excellence as through humanity.

COMMUNITY

MASJID

The mosque committee oversees the spiritual activities of the club, including Quranic sessions, maintenance of the school's mosque, organization of Tajweed and Sirâh competitions, and hosting conferences featuring distinguished Moroccan scholars during the event "FI RIHAB ALHABIB."

SOCIAL

The social committee organizes all activities with the theme of volunteering and charity. Its operations can be in the form of awareness days, caravans charitable, etc.

CULTURE

The cultural committee organizes any event or action having a cultural background and artistic. This can take the form of event days, conferences or any other suitable format.

MISSION

Our mission revolves around the idea of serving, learning, and growing together. The club is founded on the principles of action, commitment, and sharing.

1

Contribute to the active, responsible, and engaged former engineering student, aware of their role in life

2

Promote the moral and relational development of engineering students

3

Enhance cultural knowledge, skills, and interpersonal abilities of engineering students

4

Organize impactful social actions
